Strat. 1 - Rotation

Players rotate between attacking, supporting, and defending to ensure someone is always ready to challenge, follow up, or protect the goal, keeping gameplay structured and preventing chaos.

Strat. 2 - Boost Management

Collecting and conserving boost strategically so you can reach the ball quickly, challenge effectively, maintain control during plays, and recover efficiently without being stranded at low speed.

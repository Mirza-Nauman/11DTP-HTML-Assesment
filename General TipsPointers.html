Master Basic Mechanics

Practice free play to improve your ability to hit the ball with power and accuracy.

Improve Game Sense

Rotate back to defense after challenging the ball, and understand when to challenge and when to let a teammate take the play.

Play As A Team

Don't both go for the same ball. Communicate or watch your teammate's positioning to avoid collisions.

Enjoy Playing!

Have fun while playing and don't get too discouraged by losses. Learning takes time and practice.
